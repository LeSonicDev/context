## curriculum

- **EN**: the set of subjects, content and skills a school plans for students to learn
    
- **中**：课程设置；教学大纲
    
- **搭配**：national **curriculum**／core **curriculum**／hidden **curriculum**／**curriculum** design／**curriculum** mapping
    
- **例句**：Our school’s junior secondary **curriculum** blends STEM with arts to build balanced skills.
    

## pedagogy

- **EN**: methods and principles of teaching and learning
    
- **中**：教学法；教育学方法论
    
- **搭配**：student-centred **pedagogy**／evidence-based **pedagogy**／digital **pedagogy**／language **pedagogy**
    
- **例句**：The English panel promotes student-centred **pedagogy** such as project-based learning.
    

## formative assessment

- **EN**: ongoing checks to inform teaching and help students improve
    
- **中**：形成性评估（边学边评、促学）
    
- **搭配**：**formative assessment** cycle／feedback／rubrics／tasks
    
- **例句**：Weekly quizzes serve as **formative assessment** and guide our revision.
    

## summative assessment

- **EN**: end-of-unit or end-term evaluation of learning
    
- **中**：终结性评估（单元/学期末）
    
- **搭配**：**summative assessment** results／exam／grade／standard
    
- **例句**：The term exam is a **summative assessment** of all the reading skills taught.
    

## literacy

- **EN**: the ability to read and write; extended to media/data competence
    
- **中**：读写能力；（引申）媒介/数据素养
    
- **搭配**：reading **literacy**／media **literacy**／digital **literacy**／**literacy** rate
    
- **例句**：Debate club boosts critical reading **literacy**.

## scaffold / scaffolding

- **搭配**：instructional **scaffolding**／language **scaffolding**／**scaffold** tasks／**scaffolded** support
