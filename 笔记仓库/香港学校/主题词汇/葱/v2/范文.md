# 4) Technology & Digital Life

Our project uses an **algorithm** and lightweight **automation** to turn air-quality **analytics** and on-device **diagnostics** into alerts shown through tactile **interactivity** and a clean **interface** on a **wearable** badge. With strong **cybersecurity**, including **encryption** and strict limits on **surveillance**, we safeguard **biometrics** and reduce **misinformation** risk. To bridge the **digital divide** and curb **algorithmic bias**, we tested the system on diverse data and measured network **latency**. In the lab **demonstration**, a **webcam** and **headphones** link to an **antenna** and a low-power **chip** to **detect** sound patterns; a **high-pitched** alarm aligns to a warning **chord**, its **harmonic** profile shown in musical **notation**. A mapping **drone** verifies readings over hard-to-reach areas, while a **diagnostic** screen explains errors clearly.
